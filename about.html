About Guru Repair

We're on a mission to modernize repair businesses around the world, starting from our roots in South Asia.

Our Story

Guru Repair was born out of frustration. Our founders noticed that while repair shops were fixing cutting-edge technology, they were still running their businesses on pen, paper, and messy spreadsheets.

We set out to build a platform that didn't just digitize tickets, but actually streamlined the entire workflow—from intake to invoicing. Today, Guru Repair powers thousands of repair transactions daily, bringing order to the beautiful chaos of the repair industry.

Our Mission

To empower independent repair businesses with enterprise-grade tools that are simple, accessible, and designed to save time so they can focus on what they do best: fixing things.

Our Vision

A world where the circular economy thrives, electronic waste is minimized, and local repair shops are the trusted heroes in every community.

Local Roots. Global Reach.

Built with a deep understanding of the unique challenges faced by businesses in Nepal and South Asia, yet architected to scale for repair operations worldwide.

South Asian Context

We natively integrate with local payment gateways like eSewa and Khalti, and understand local tax and invoicing compliance requirements inside-out.

Global Infrastructure

Hosted on world-class cloud infrastructure ensuring 99.9% uptime, blazing fast performance, and bank-grade security no matter where your shop is located.

Community First

We actively support the Right to Repair movement globally while sponsoring local tech education initiatives in our home region.
